Let $S_m = (x_1, x_2, \dots , x_m)$ be the $m$-tuple of positive real numbers with $x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_m = m$ for which $P_m = x_1 \cdot x_2^2 \cdot \cdots \cdot x_m^m$ is maximised.
