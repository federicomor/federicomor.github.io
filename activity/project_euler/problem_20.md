<p>For example, $10! = 10 \times 9 \times \cdots \times 3 \times 2 \times 1 = 3628800$,<br>and the sum of the digits in the number $10!$ is $3 + 6 + 2 + 8 + 8 + 0 + 0 = 27$.</p>
