The ten numbers not exceeding $100$ having exactly eight divisors are $24, 30, 40, 42, 54, 56, 66, 70, 78$ and $88$.

You are given $f(100) = 10$, $f(1000) = 180$ and $f(10^6) = 224427$.<br>
