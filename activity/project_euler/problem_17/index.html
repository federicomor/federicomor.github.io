(P17) Number Letter Counts

If the numbers 111 to 555 are written out in words: one, two, three, four, five, then there are 3+3+5+4+4=193 + 3 + 5 + 4 + 4 = 193+3+5+4+4=19 letters used in total. If all the numbers from 111 to 100010001000 (one thousand) inclusive were written out in words, how many letters would be used? <p class="note"><b>NOTE:</b> Do not count spaces or hyphens. For example, 342342342 (three hundred and forty-two) contains 232323 letters and 115115115 (one hundred and fifteen) contains 202020 letters. The use of "and" when writing out numbers is in compliance with British usage.
