<p>
$$\sum_{j=0}^m\sum_{i = 0}^j (-1)^{j-i}\binom mj \binom ji \binom{j+5+6i}{j+5}.$$
</p>
<p>

Find the first ten digits of $g(142857)$ when written in base $7$.
