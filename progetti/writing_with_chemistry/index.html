Writing with chemistry

This project dates back to high school years where, inspired by the periodic table hang on the wall of the classroom, I tried to build words and phrases using as letters the elements from the periodic table. At the time I implemented this idea with some C code, which is available here, but now I also provide here a conversion to javascript.
